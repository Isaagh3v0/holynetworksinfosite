Deleting data from Holy Networks

To remove your data from the Holy Networks website, you must send an email request to support@Holy Networks.tech. The request must include the following information:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your e-mail address
  • User ID (if available)

Upon receipt of your request, Holy Networks staff will review the request and remove all of your data from the system within 30 days.

Example request:

Dear Holy Networks staff, i am requesting that my data be removed from the Holy Networks website.

  • My first and last name: [Your first and last name].
  • My e-mail address: [Your e-mail address].
  • My User ID: [User ID (if available)].

Thank you for your attention.

Additional Information:

  • You must be a registered Holy Networks user to delete your data.
  • If you do not know your User ID, you can find it in your profile settings.
  • If you do not receive a response to your request within 30 days, you may resubmit your request or contact Holy Networks at +994 10 322 65 55.
